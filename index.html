Electro Swing-

Electro Swing is music genre that combines vintage Swing with ‘Electronic’ music (House, EDM, Funk, Hip Hop and so on). Electro Swing use a lot of loop and sample from the Swing tracks (Swing, Jazz and the Big Band era). The genre saw began being successful in the 90s with Hip Hop influenced tracks it haven’t got the name ‘Electro Sing’ name yet but in 2000 it began to take shape and got some new names ‘Swing hop’, ‘Swing Set’, ‘Nu-Jazz’ and so on in circa 2010 it got its name ‘Electro Swing’. Electro Swing artist is located like most indie in UK, France and US (not all but most) and host on SoundCloud, Beatspot and Juno.
